DESCRIPTION

Players simply pick up 1 to 8 numbers from 80 numbers in total.

The system randomly draws 20 numbers. With a game running as often as every 2 minutes, the potential hits are countless!

GAMING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Choose your numbers (up to 8) using the grid or the option “Quick Bet”
  2. Select your amount using the tokens with predefined values on the right side of the betting interface, you can combine them to bet the desired amount.
  3. When your selection is done, use the “Validate” button to place your bets.
