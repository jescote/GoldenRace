DESCRIPTION

With the simplicity and relatively casual form of play that comes from having just 12 numbers, mini roulette is growing to be an increasingly popular variant of classic roulette.

The game features an European with zero and double zero. This is the same roulette as its real Casino counterpart with less number to play on, making it easier for player to use his/her strategy on a fast pace roulette action.

GAMING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Select a token (jetons) and tap on the option you want to bet on.
  2. If you tap repeatedly, the amount will be increased depending on the value of the selected token.
  3. When your selection is done, use the “Validate” button to place your bets.

BET TYPES

TYPE DEFINITION SYMBOL
STRAIGHT You select one number (from 1 to 12). -
SPLIT Bet on a combination of two numbers (e.g.: 1-2, 1-4, 2-5…)
STREET Bet on a combination of three numbers (e.g: 1-2-3, 4-5-6…)
CORNER Bet on a combination of four numbers.
COLUMN Bet on a column of four numbers.
HALF DOZENS Bet on one of the following half dozens (e.g: 1 to 6, 4 to 9...).
RED / BLACK Bet on the color of the cell.
EVEN / ODD Choose whether the drawn number will be odd or even.
